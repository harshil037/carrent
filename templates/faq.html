FAQ

How old do I need to be to rent a Car from Avis?

You can rent an Avis Self Drive car if you are 18 years or above.

How can I reserve a car?

With Avis Flex, allows you to get a price benefit when you book for 7 days and above on select car models. Rentals start for as low as Rs 1250/day.

Do I need to pick up the car from Avis rental office?

All the cars are delivered and collected free of cost to the address specified at the time of making a booking. Note; cars will not be delivered to any public places like malls, metro station, etc.

Avis holds the right to cancel the booking in such case.

I need to drop off the car at a different location, can I do that? ?

No. The car will be collected by our driver from the place to where it was delivered.

What is the fuel policy ?

As per the Avis fuel policy:

  • The car will be delivered with a full tank and need to be returned in the same manner.

  • Customer pays for the fuel charges based on the usage.

  • If the car is returned with less than full tank fuel, Avis will charge for the differential fuel.

  • The customer can either pay this directly by cash/card at the time of car pick up by Avis driver or the same will be deducted from the security deposit.

  • The assessment made by the Avis driver on the differential fuel amount will be final.

What if I return the car late ?

Guest needs to inform Avis in advance for rental extension. An extension or grace period of up to 1 hours is permissible if informed with no extra charges. If the extension exceeds 1 hours, following charges will be applicable for every hour including the 1-hour grace period.

To avoid paying any extension fee we request our guest to extend the booking through the Avis India website or mobile app.
